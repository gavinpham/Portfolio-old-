My name is Gavin Pham, and I am some kind of whacky hybridization between a computer scientist and a human-centered designer.

Over the past couple of years, have been honing my skills as a design researcher within the Masters of Arts in Design and Innovation program at Southern Methodist Univeristy. I have conducted hundreds of research interviews, written a ludicrous amount of sticky notes, and prototyped some really awesome ideas.

My second love is devoted to coding and computer science. I received my Bachelor's in Computer Science at SMU in May of 2017. I cofounded HackDFW, one of the largest hackathon organizations in the United States.

When the situation allows it, I will nerd out on sorting efficiency and optimization. That's why all of the animations on this site are written in CSS.

These two areas of interest, human centered design and computer science, occasionally intersect. That is where I find my happy place.
