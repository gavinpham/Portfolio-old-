--- layout: discipline title: "Rapid Prototyping" permalink: /disciplines/pro category: ['discipline'] tag: "pro" icon: "fa fa-rocket" description: | Rapid prototyping defines a fast, iterative approach to refining a solution.

Prototyping is a great tool as a means of testing the merit of an idea. Constructing a low-fidelity version of an solution can help to answer a lot of questions, as well as give rise to conversations about assumptions being made.

Rapidly adding small improvements to a prototype is where I find the most value in prototyping.
