Human-centered design has been a foundational element in my graduate work. I absolutely love the process. Years of experience and repetition of its steps has helped me to learned how to thrive in uncertainty. I've interviewed hundreds of people for their perspectives on laundry, dogs, public transit, pharmaceuticals; you name it, I've probably researched it. Synthesis and ideation around learned information has helped myself and my teams to move closer to truly beneficial solutions. Rapid prototyping and testing breathes life to ideas and highlights its strengths and weaknesses.
